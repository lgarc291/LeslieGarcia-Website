Leslie Garcia

Liberal Studies Major

I am a full-time student who is attending the University of California Riverside. I am currently working towards a Bachelors degree in Liberal Studies. After receiving a Bachelors Degree I hope to apply and work to retain a multi-subject teaching credential. My goal is to become an elementary school teacher to work with children in a low income elementary school.

I enjoy working in a creative environment, especially with kids. I have volunteered in classrooms before, however, I have never managed a group of kids on my own and I would like to have that experience to learn. Over time, opportunities to teach and lend help without anything in return have significantly been instilled through my religion. I live my life through this principle by helping those who are younger than me. I value establishing an approachable demeanor highlighting patience and kindness. I realized my caring instinct would be fundamental to my goals in a teaching career. Assessing the situation and managing to provide some sort of support and understanding for those kids was instinctive.

I enjoy film photography and taking pictures of random fun moments. I play tennis and I enjoy playing volleyball on my free time and watching baseball games occasionally. I enjoy hiking, right now my favorite trail is the Heart Rock Trail near Silverwood lake.
